## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is My Bible Log?

My Bible Log is a free online Bible reading tracker that helps you track what you’ve read and when you read it. Instead of following a predefined reading plan, you log your actual Bible reading and see your progress over time.

---

### Is My Bible Log free?

Yes. My Bible Log is completely free to use.

* No ads
* No subscriptions
* No paid features

You can sign up with your email address or use Google sign-in to get started.

---

### Do I have to follow a Bible reading plan?

No. My Bible Log does not assign reading plans or schedules.

You read the Bible however you choose—through sermons, devotionals, personal study, or other resources—and log what you’ve read. As long as you’re reading new verses, all of it counts toward your progress.

---

### Can My Bible Log help me read the Bible in a year?

Yes. One of the main goals of My Bible Log is to help people read the entire Bible.

* The Bible contains **31,102 verses**
* Reading an average of **86 new verses per day** allows you to finish in one year
* You can read more on some days and less on others—it all balances out over time

My Bible Log tracks your pace and helps you stay aware of how close you are to your goal.

---

### How do daily reading goals work?

You can set a daily goal based on how many **new verses** you want to read each day.

Each time you log reading, My Bible Log updates your progress toward that goal. Days where you meet your goal are marked with a **gold star** on your reading calendar.

---

### Can I set a target date to finish the Bible?

Yes. You can choose a date when you want to finish reading the Bible.

My Bible Log will automatically calculate how many verses you need to read each day to reach that goal by your chosen date.

---

### What are reading suggestions?

Reading suggestions help you decide what to read next.

Suggestions are based on:

* What you’ve already logged
* Which chapters you haven’t read yet
* Your recent reading activity

They make it easy to keep moving through the Bible without needing a formal plan.

---

### How do I log my Bible reading?

Logging is designed to be quick and simple.

You can:

* Select a book, chapter, and verse range
* Log entire chapters
* Mark chapters as read with a single click from the Chapter Checklist

Each log entry records what you read and when you read it.

---

### Can I track progress by chapter and book?

Yes.

You can:

* See which chapters you’ve read in each book
* View progress bars for each book of the Bible
* See your overall progress across the entire Bible

Chapters you’ve read are clearly marked so you can easily find unread sections.

---

### Does My Bible Log include a Bible translation?

My Bible Log does not include its own Bible text.

Instead, it provides direct links to external Bible apps and websites. The most commonly used Bible translations are supported, and additional translations continue to be added.

---

### Can I take notes on my Bible reading?

Yes. My Bible Log includes a built-in notes feature.

You can use notes to:

* Record insights from your reading
* Write down questions to revisit later
* Keep a personal study record tied to your reading

---

### Can I organize my notes?

Yes. Notes can be organized using **custom tags**.

You can:

* Create your own tags
* Assign custom colors
* Use tags for topics, memory verses, questions, or study themes

This allows you to organize notes in a way that fits your personal study style.

---

### Can I view my reading history?

Yes. The Reading Calendar shows your Bible reading activity over time.

You can:

* View and edit any log entry
* See trends in your reading habits
* Identify days when you met your daily goal

---

### Can I export or import my reading data?

Yes.

You can export your reading log as a spreadsheet, or import a spreadsheet to quickly get started or restore your data.

---

### Can I use My Bible Log on my phone or computer?

Yes. My Bible Log works on modern phones, tablets, and desktop computers.

You can use it in a web browser or install it as an app on your device for convenient access.

If you need help installing the app, search the web for "How to install a Progressive Web App on (your device)"

---

### Do I need to create a password?

No. You can sign in using Google without creating a password.

If you prefer, you can also create an account using your email address, which will allow you to sign in with a password.

---

### Is my reading data private?

Yes. Your reading data is private and visible only to you.

My Bible Log has no social features and does not share your reading activity with other users.

---

### Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. You can delete your account at any time.

Account deletion:

* Immediately and permanently deletes all your data
* Cannot be undone
* Removes all reading logs, notes, and settings

---

### Is My Bible Log designed for group or church use?

My Bible Log is currently designed for **personal Bible reading and study**.

It does not include social, group, or sharing features.

This is intentional, helping to promote focused personal study and keep My Bible Log safe for users of all ages.
